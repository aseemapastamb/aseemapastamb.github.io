Summary

Between January and April of 2023, as part of my Master's course, I developed a 3D game with a team. The game is called DTBB, short for Drifty Thrifty Bang Bang, and it is a 3D isometric driving game, packed with action and destruction.

The game was developed in a custom C++ engine that I worked on in 2022.
